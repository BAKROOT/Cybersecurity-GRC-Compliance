## 1. Purpose

This policy sets out the organization's commitment to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information and to managing information-security risk.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all information assets, personnel, contractors, systems, and third parties acting on behalf of the organization.

## 3. Policy Statements

- Information shall be protected in line with its classification and business value.
- Access to information is granted on a least-privilege, need-to-know basis.
- Risks to information assets shall be identified, assessed, and treated.
- Security requirements shall be built into projects, systems, and supplier relationships.
- Security incidents shall be reported, investigated, and learned from.
- The organization shall meet applicable legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations.
- All personnel shall receive security awareness training.

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities

The CISO owns this policy. Managers ensure their teams comply. All personnel are responsible for protecting information they handle and reporting concerns.

## 5. Compliance and Exceptions

Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action. Exceptions require documented risk acceptance approved by the CISO.

## 6. Supporting Documents

This policy is supported by the Acceptable Use, Access Control, and Data Classification policies, and by the standards and procedures in this repository.

## 7. Review

Reviewed at least annually and after significant change.
