## 1. Purpose

This policy defines acceptable and unacceptable use of organizational information systems and assets to protect users and the organization from harm.

## 2. Scope

Applies to all users of organizational systems, networks, devices, accounts, and data, including employees, contractors, and third parties.

## 3. Acceptable Use

- Use organizational assets primarily for legitimate business purposes.
- Protect credentials and lock devices when unattended.
- Report lost or stolen devices and suspected security incidents promptly.
- Store and share data only through approved systems and in line with its classification.

## 4. Unacceptable Use

- Sharing accounts or credentials with others.
- Installing unauthorized software or bypassing security controls.
- Accessing, storing, or transmitting unlawful, harassing, or offensive material.
- Using assets to harm the organization, its customers, or third parties.
- Connecting unauthorized devices to corporate networks.

## 5. Monitoring

The organization may monitor use of its systems to the extent permitted by law to ensure security and compliance. Users should have no expectation of privacy when using organizational assets.

## 6. Enforcement

Violations may result in revocation of access and disciplinary action up to and including termination, as well as legal action where appropriate.

## 7. Review

Reviewed at least annually.
